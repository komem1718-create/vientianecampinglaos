✦ Day-by-Day (Adventure Tier)

What to Expect

01

Vientiane Departure → Forest Trailhead

Meet your guide at 7 AM in central Vientiane. Transfer by 4WD to the mountain trailhead (~2 hrs). Begin trekking through bamboo forest and ascending ridge paths. Arrive at camp by late afternoon. Set up tents, gather firewood, and enjoy a traditional Lao dinner as the stars appear.

02

Summit Morning → Waterfall Side-Hike

Rise early for the sunrise from the ridge (optional guided stargazing the night before). Breakfast over the fire, then tackle the short summit push for panoramic views over Laos and distant Thailand. Afternoon detour to a hidden waterfall. Return to camp for the second night.

03

Descent → Village Stop → Vientiane

Morning coffee at camp with the mist still below you. Pack up and descend via an alternative trail through a traditional highland village, where you can browse local crafts. Return to Vientiane by 3 PM with memories that'll last a lifetime.
